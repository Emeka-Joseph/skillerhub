How Skillerhubs Works

Skillerhubs is a platform for the display of skills, both as an enterpreneur or an organisation. This display can come in different formats but most graphical. The unique term used for an enterpreneur or an organisation in the Skillerhubs Platform is called "Skiller". As a visitor to the platform, you would be able to search for skillers/skills and as well as filter your searches by location (States for now) and get a desired result but would not be able to view their contact details untill you join the community.

The platform is more like a Digital Showroom for the display of the projects you have successfully completed in anticipation of attracting more clients/customers.
Skillerhubs is not just for the skilled in terms of craftmaship, but also in terms of vocation. Once you are very skillful in rendering a service and selling of best-grade and trusted product with exceptional customer-services, you are welcome

Join

To join is absolutely free for now. When you sign up, you automatically become a user. To become a Skiller, follow the guidlines and register/update your profile in your dashboard page. When Joining/Registering as a Skiller, graphical representations of previously completed projects are displayed in the skiller's dashboard which would be seen while every other users wants to view the profile of the skiller in quote.

It is also important to note that if any account violates the policies and guidlines of the platform, Skillerhubs Admin reserves the right to suspend/delete such account with or without prior notice.
